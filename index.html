Our Special Menu

Beef Taiwan Marinated

The menu takes a contemporary twist on classic, home-style cooking with moderate prices. Seasoned Belgian Chef Benoit Claeys prepares a diverse range of dishes which include not only bistro favorites such as Salad Nicoise, Roast Chicken Rotisserie and Steak Frites, but also European-style curry, a selection of simple but hearty pasta dishes, even Asian inspired Oriental Chicken Salad that brings the palate a little closer to home. And then too are the desserts: a variety of tarts with the freshest tropical fruits in season, and of course, Chef Benoit Claeys' legendary Mille-Feuille.

our team



Vincenzo Ziko

FOUNDER / GENERAL MANAGER

Vincenzo Zilko is our full time general manager. He creates the concept of Raisin Restaurant together with his partner Mr. Aurick (currently fulltime restaurant manager). He is responsible of whole Raisin Restaurant and Catering, and focus on creating marketing plan, quality control, developing new menu, and running the whole business.


With his background as Industrial Engineer, he can maximize the efficiency and effectivity of the kitchen system. Minimalizing waste and creating the most efficient serving system while also conducting the great ambience in the dining room is what he is capable of. Based on his experience working in big city such as Jakarta and Bandung, he knows what it has to do to create the hypest restaurant in town.
